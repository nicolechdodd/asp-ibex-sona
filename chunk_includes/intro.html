Title of study: Psycholinguistic Study of Understanding Sentence
Investigator: Dr. Emily Morgan, Assistant Professor of Linguistics, University of California, Davis

Introduction and Purpose
You are being invited to join a research study. If you agree to participate in this research, you will be asked to read everyday words and/or sentences. Your reading time may be recorded using either button presses or an eye-tracker with a camera. You may be asked to respond to what you read in either a multiple-choice or an open-ended format, either by typing or by responding verbally. Your responses will be anonymous. Your participation in this research should take about 30-90 minutes.

Participation in research is completely voluntary. You are free to decline to take part in the project. You can decline to answer any questions and you can stop taking part in the project at any time. Whether or not you choose to participate, or answer any question, or stop participating in the project, there will be no penalty to you or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

If you agree to take part in this research study, we will compensate you .5 SONA credit per 30 minutes of participation for your time and effort.
